About Me

I am a Fellow Member of ICSI. My other qualifications include B.Com., LL.B., and Certified Independent Director from the Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs (IICA).

With 8+ years of experience, I specialize in secretarial practice, legal compliance, SEBI listing regulations, IPR protection, and corporate governance. I am currently practicing as a Company Secretary under the firm name Ketan Vyas & Co.

My firm is a Peer Reviewed Firm registered with ICSI and certified under its Peer Review Guidelines, reflecting our dedication to professional excellence, ethical conduct, and quality control.

We are authorized to carry out regulatory and compliance-related assignments, including Secretarial Audits for listed companies and corporate governance advisory, where peer review certification is a prerequisite.
